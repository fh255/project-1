Reasons to visit us

Stunning Natural Beauty

Cox's Bazar boasts breathtaking landscapes with its long sandy beach, rolling hills, and the Bay of Bengal. The picturesque setting is a major draw for tourists.Many people are attracted to the peaceful and serene environment that Cox's Bazar offers. It's an ideal place for relaxation and escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.The beach is known for its spectacular sunsets and sunrises, creating magical moments for visitors to cherish

Seafood Delicacies

The town is famous for its seafood, and people visit Cox's Bazar to savor fresh and delicious fish and seafood dishes.The unpolluted brackish waters of the Bay of Bengal are home of various species of marine shrimps and demersal fish. Among marine shrimps, major species in terms of volume of yield from catches, landings and aquaculture are the giant tiger shrimps and yellow shrimps.

Adventure Activities

The area offers various adventure sports such as surfing, Water sport, parasailing, beach camping,trekking and hiking, fishing, motor biking, cycling, rock climbing making it appealing to adventure enthusiasts.This all activities are super budget friendly.

Coral Reefs

Nearby Saint Martin's Island is famous for its coral reefs, making it a paradise for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts. The other beaches like Inani Beach , Kuakata, Kutubdia Island: are also famous in Cox's bazar.
